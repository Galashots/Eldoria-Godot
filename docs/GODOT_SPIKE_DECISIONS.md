# Godot Spike Decisions

- Engine: Godot 4.x standard.
- Language: GDScript.
- Scope: a Godot spike, not an Eldoria-V2 replacement yet.
- Design rule: use Godot-native scenes and nodes.
- Porting rule: do not blindly port V2; preserve useful design intent and rebuild small vertical slices in Godot-native form.
